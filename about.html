 About Us 

History

Hitesh Rajpurohit

Our journey at Rajlaxmi Steel Trading Shop began with the vision of our esteemed founder, Hitesh Rajpurohit . With a profound understanding of the steel industry and a passion for serving the local community, Hitesh Rajpurohit laid the foundation for our establishment. Their extensive experience and entrepreneurial spirit have been instrumental in shaping our company into what it is today. Through their leadership and unwavering commitment to excellence, we continue to uphold the values of integrity, reliability, and customer satisfaction that Hitesh Rajpurohit instilled in us from the outset.

When you choose to work with Rajlaxmi Steel Trader's, you choose to work with...

An experienced, qualified peoples which will understand your requirements, and guide you to steel specifications that best suit your needs.

A franchise dedicated to providing best quality steel and outstanding service to its clients, each and every time.

Leadership that keeps striving to develop and expand the franchise in order to give their clients a better experience.

Our Vision

At Rajlaxmi steel Trader's, we've been a trusted name in local steel trading since past years. Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has been the cornerstone of our business for many years. As a family-owned establishment, we take pride in our roots within the community and our dedication to providing top-notch steel products and services. Our experienced team is here to assist you with all your steel needs, ensuring that you receive personalized attention and expert guidance every step of the way.
